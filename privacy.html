Privacy Policy for PrivCite

Effective Date: May 28, 2026
Last Updated: May 28, 2026

PrivCite is a local-only macOS application designed to help law students, law clerks, summer associates, and attorneys review legal documents for Bluebook citation compliance and writing style issues.

Data Collection and Use

PrivCite collects no personal data or document content whatsoever.

  • All document analysis, issue detection, redlining, and export operations occur entirely on your device using only local Apple frameworks and pure Swift code.
  • Your documents, memos, briefs, and any generated review or fixed copies never leave your Mac.
  • The app makes zero outbound network connections during normal operation.
  • There are no accounts, no telemetry, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party tracking or advertising.
  • (If any optional future local-only LLM features are added, they would communicate exclusively with software you choose to run on your own machine, such as Ollama on localhost.)

Your Rights

Because PrivCite never receives or stores any of your information, there is nothing for us to access, delete, or correct on our end. You retain complete control over your work at all times.

Third-Party Services

PrivCite contains no third-party analytics, advertising, data-sharing, or cloud components.

Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy only if the app's fundamental behavior changes. The effective date will be revised accordingly. Past analyses performed on your machine are unaffected.
